Telekom Albania

PR & Communications, Digital Solutions 2023

Overview

Telekom Albania, one of the country's leading telecommunications providers, needed to revitalize their brand image and improve customer perception in a highly competitive market. We developed and executed a comprehensive rebranding strategy that involved multiple channels and touchpoints.

Challenges

  • Declining market share due to increased competition
  • Negative customer perception regarding service quality
  • Outdated brand identity that didn't reflect the company's innovative services
  • Need to communicate complex offerings in a simple, appealing way

  • Created an integrated communications campaign across digital, print, and broadcast media
  • Implemented a social media strategy focused on customer engagement and service improvement

Wizz Air Launches

Event Management, Media Buying 2024

Overview

Wizz Air, a leading European low-cost airline, engaged our agency to create awareness and generate bookings for several new route launches in the Balkan region. We developed a multi-channel campaign that successfully positioned these routes and drove significant booking numbers.

Challenges

  • Limited awareness of Wizz Air in some target markets
  • Need to target different audiences across multiple countries
  • Strong competition from established carriers on key routes
  • Tight timeline for campaign development and execution

Solutions

  • Created a unified campaign concept adaptable to different cultural contexts
  • Implemented targeted digital advertising across search, social, and display networks
  • Organized press events at airports for each new route launch
  • Developed partnerships with tourism boards and local businesses
  • Executed outdoor advertising in key locations around departure and destination cities
