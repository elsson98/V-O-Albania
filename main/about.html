Who Are We

Founded in 2011, V+O Albania is a trailblazer in integrated communication services, pioneering PR and digital innovation in a market ripe with potential. From our early work rebranding Telekom Albania to managing high-profile launches for Wizz Air and Wolt, we’ve built a reputation for excellence, agility, and deep industry connections. Our team thrives on challenges, turning complex briefs into campaigns that resonate across media, boardrooms, and communities.

Mission

To redefine communication through strategic creativity. We partner with clients to build narratives that inspire action, foster trust, and amplify visibility—whether through crisis management, experiential events, or cutting-edge digital campaigns.

Vision

We envision a world where brands don’t just speak but connect. By merging Albanian market insights with global best practices, we’re shaping a future where every touch-point is an opportunity for growth.
